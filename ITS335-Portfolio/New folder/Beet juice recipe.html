Healthy Beet Juice

Do you have itchy, dry eyes and sinusitis? Then, Grab a juicer, put beetroot in your grocery list. They are packed with so many vitamins and minerals. It may save you money from medicine and trip to doctor's office.

TOOL NEEDED

  • juicer
  • cutting board
  • knife

Ingredients

  • 1/4 to 1/2 beetroot ( If this is your first time start small)
  • 1 to 2 Carrot (You can add more if you want it sweeter)
  • Half to 1 stalk Celery ( I only use ½ of it; I would like the taste of carrot than celery)
  • Fruits like apple, pineapple, pear or other fruit to make this taste better.

Instructions

  1. Cut the ingredients that will fit in your juicer.
  2. Then, start juicing according to your juicer's instructions, pour to glass container.
  3. Enjoy the drink. If this is for 2 people split the drink.

Modification

  1. Add your favorite fruit to tone down the earthy floavor of beets.
